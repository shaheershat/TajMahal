About the Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is one of the most iconic monuments in the world. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it stands as a symbol of love and architectural beauty. Made from white marble, the mausoleum attracts millions of visitors each year.
