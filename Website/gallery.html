Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit the Taj Mahal is during the cooler months from October to March. The weather is pleasant, making it ideal for sightseeing. Early mornings and evenings provide the best lighting for photography, with fewer crowds. Visiting during the full moon night offers a magical view of the monument bathed in moonlight.

How to Reach

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, and is well connected by road, rail, and air:

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Agra Airport, about 13 km away. Indira Gandhi International Airport (Delhi) is another major option, around 200 km away.
  • By Train: Agra Cantt is the main railway station, with regular trains from major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Jaipur.
  • By Road: Agra is well connected via NH-19 and the Yamuna Expressway, making it accessible from Delhi (about 3-4 hours by car).

Travel Safety Tips

  • Carry a valid ID for entry.
  • Avoid visiting during peak summer (April–June).
  • Beware of unauthorized guides; use official ones.
  • Do not carry large bags; security checks are strict.
